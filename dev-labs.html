My Goals

I plan to get out of DevPoint Labs the ability to code in Javascript at a level that I could impress a potential employer. I’m not sure at this point if I’m going to apply for a job when I’m through with the program but the knowledge I gain will be valuable to my own business as well as making me more competitive applying in other businesses.

I also think that who you associate with can be one of the most valuable learning and growing opportunities. I feel like this bootcamp will put me in the presence of likeminded people giving me the opportunity to teach and learn amongst peers.
