About Me

I graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Communications in 2014 with an emphasis in advertising and video production. I spent 2 years working for the ad agency at Brigham Young University- Idaho called Soapbox. At Soapbox I created video ads for various local businesses.

After graduation, I got a job at Construx, a software best practices consulting company. While there, I created online video courses for training software teams. At the same time, I started to learn basic web development (html and css) and eventually got heavily involved in creating wordpress based websites (pre-built themes as well as 100% custom themes) and offering wordpress site maintenace services.

In my free time I like to watch movies and tv shows, start projects (and sometimes finish), read, think, study, and spend time with my wife McKenna and my little girl Molly.
